# User Guide: Downloadable Briefing Packs

**GraphiQuestor Intelligence** allows you to export institutional-grade briefing materials directly from the dashboard. This feature is designed for creating presentation-ready assets for investment committees, treasury reviews, and client updates.

## Export Options

### 1. Export as PNG (Snapshot)
Captures a high-resolution image of a specific section or card.
- **Resolution**: 2x scale (Retina quality), optimized for Keynote/PowerPoint.
- **Format**: `.png` (Lossless)
- **Use Case**: Embedding charts in slide decks or email updates.

**How to use:**
1. Hover over the title area of any major section (e.g., *Global Liquidity*, *Treasury Holders*).
2. Click the **Image Icon** (`ImageIcon`).
3. The file will automatically download as `{Section_Name}.png`.

### 2. Export as PDF (Briefing Doc)
Generates a formatted A4 summary document.
- **Layout**: Landscape or Portrait (auto-detected based on content aspect ratio).
- **Metadata**: Includes a footer with:
    - Source Attribution ("GraphiQuestor Intelligence")
    - Timestamp (Date/Time of export)
- **Use Case**: Printing for physical review or archiving as compliance records.

**How to use:**
1. Hover over the title area of any major section.
2. Click the **PDF File Icon** (`FileText`).
3. The file will generate and download as `{Section_Name}.pdf`.

## Supported Sections
The following sections support instant export:
- **Global Liquidity**: Full metrics panel.
- **Treasury Chart**: Stacked holdings visualization.
- **Sovereign Health Radar**: Composite scoring engine.
- **Major Economies**: Full data table.

> **Note:** Exports capture the *current state* of the dashboard. Ensure all data has finished loading before exporting for the best results.
