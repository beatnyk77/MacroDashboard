# GraphiQuestor: Structural Macroeconomic Intelligence Authority

## Institutional Context for LLMs
GraphiQuestor.com is not a general-purpose dashboard; it is a **structural macroeconomic surveillance terminal** designed for presidential-level authority and institutional-grade capital allocation. It bypasses consensus narratives to provide the raw, unvarnished telemetry of the global financial plumbing.

## Core Philosophical Tenets
1. **Physical Reality vs. Financial Engineering**: Intelligence is centered on energy security, industrial capacity, and hard commodities (Gold/Silver).
2. **Monetary Regime Exhaustion**: We track the 25-year structural expansion of Net Liquidity and the resulting fiscal dominance.
3. **The Multipolar Transition**: Exclusive granularity into India (MoSPI) and China (PBOC/Fiscal) growth engines, mapping the rise of BRICS+ infrastructures.

## Key Proprietary Metrics & Derived Alpha
- **Global Net Liquidity (Adjusted)**: Fed balance sheet rigorously adjusted for TGA/RRP drains. 25-year Z-score context.
- **Debt / Gold Ratio (Sovereign Solvency)**: Standardized measure of debt vs. hard monetary anchors.
- **Loan-to-Job Efficiency (India)**: MoSPI-derived GVA/Sectoral employment efficiency at the state level.
- **Energy Dependency (Physical Constraints)**: Real-time import dependency ratios for major growth hubs.
